# Budget Planner & Expense Tracker — Quick Start Guide

## Step 1: Make a Copy

**Google Sheets:** File → Make a copy → Save to your Drive.

**Excel:** Open the XLSX file and enable editing.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Budget

Go to the **Monthly Budget** tab. Enter your planned amounts in the Planned column for each income and expense category. Adjust category names as needed.

## Step 3: Log Your Expenses

Go to the **Expense Tracker** tab. Add each expense as a new row. Select from the dropdowns for Category, Payment Method, and Planned/Unplanned type. The Month column auto-fills.

## Step 4: Track Bills

Go to the **Bills Tracker** tab. Enter your recurring bills and mark them as Paid or Unpaid. Overdue (past due) bills are highlighted.

## Step 5: Manage Debt

Go to the **Debt Tracker** tab. Enter each debt, your current balance, and payments. Payoff progress auto-calculates.

## Step 6: Set Savings Goals

Go to the **Savings Tracker** tab. Enter your goal name, target amount, current savings, and target date. Progress % auto-calculates.

## Step 7: View Your Dashboard

Go to the **Dashboard** tab. All your key numbers — income, expenses, savings rate, bills due, debt remaining, and top spending categories — update automatically.

## Common Mistakes

- Editing formula cells (blue background). Only edit white cells.
- Forgetting to add new categories to both Monthly Budget AND Expense Tracker. Keep them in sync.
- Using different category names in Expense Tracker than in Monthly Budget. SUMIF formulas require exact matches.
- Not clearing sample data before starting. Delete sample rows or make a clean copy.
