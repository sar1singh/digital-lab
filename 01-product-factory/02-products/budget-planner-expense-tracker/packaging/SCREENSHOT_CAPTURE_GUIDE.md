## General Screenshot Tips

- Use **clean sample data** — do not capture with empty tabs
- Ensure **all formulas have calculated** before capturing
- **Crop** screenshots to remove browser chrome, toolbars, and empty space
- Use **consistent width** across all screenshots for a professional listing
- **No personal or financial information** should appear in screenshots
- File format: PNG (preferred) or JPG at high quality
- Resolution: minimum 1280px wide for Gumroad listing images
