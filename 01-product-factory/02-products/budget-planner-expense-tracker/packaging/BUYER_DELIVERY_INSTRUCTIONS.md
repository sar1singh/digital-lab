# Buyer Delivery Instructions — Budget Planner & Expense Tracker

## What You Receive

After purchase, you will receive:
1. **Google Sheet copy link** — click to copy the spreadsheet to your Google Drive
2. **XLSX file** — downloadable spreadsheet for Microsoft Excel
3. **PDF quick-start guide** — 1-page setup instructions

## How to Access the Google Sheets Version

1. Click the **/copy link** from your purchase receipt
2. Sign in to your Google account
3. Click **Make a copy**
4. The spreadsheet appears in your Google Drive
5. Open it from Google Drive or Google Sheets home

The /copy link creates your own private copy. You can edit it freely without affecting the original.

## How to Use the XLSX Version

1. Download the XLSX file from your purchase receipt
2. Open with:
   - **Microsoft Excel** (recommended for full formula support)
   - **LibreOffice Calc** (free alternative)
   - **Apple Numbers** (basic support)
3. If Excel shows **Protected View**, click **Enable Editing**

## How to Use the Spreadsheet

1. **Monthly Budget tab** — enter your planned income and expenses in the Planned Amount column
2. **Expense Tracker tab** — add each expense as it happens (date, category, amount)
3. **Bills Tracker tab** — enter recurring bills and mark them as paid or unpaid
4. **Debt Tracker tab** — enter your debts and track payoff progress
5. **Savings Tracker tab** — set savings goals and watch progress grow
6. **Dashboard tab** — view your complete financial picture (auto-calculated)

## Important: Avoid Editing Formula Cells

Cells with **gray background** contain formulas. Do not edit them. Editing formula cells will break the spreadsheet calculations.

Only edit cells with **white or green background**. These are input cells.

Formula cells include:
- Actual Amount column in Monthly Budget
- Difference column in Monthly Budget
- Month column in Expense Tracker (auto-fills from Date)
- Payoff Progress % in Debt Tracker
- Progress % in Savings Tracker
- All Dashboard summary values
