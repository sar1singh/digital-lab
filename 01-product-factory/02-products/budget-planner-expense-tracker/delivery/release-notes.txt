Budget Planner & Expense Tracker — Release Notes

Thank you for purchasing the Budget Planner & Expense Tracker!

What you received:
- Google Sheets copy link (make a copy to start using immediately)
- Excel workbook (.xlsx) for offline use
- Quick-start guide

Features — 7 tabs in one workbook:

Start Here
  Setup instructions, navigation guide, and checklist to get started.

Monthly Budget
  Plan your income and expenses by category. See planned vs actual amounts with automatic variance tracking.

Expense Tracker
  Log every expense with date, category, payment method, and notes. Categories auto-populate into your budget.

Bills Tracker
  Track recurring bills, due dates, and payment status. Unpaid bills are highlighted for attention.

Debt Tracker
  Monitor multiple debts with starting balance, current balance, and payoff progress percentage.

Savings Tracker
  Set savings goals and track progress toward each target.

Dashboard
  At-a-glance summary: income, expenses, net savings, savings rate, bills due, debt remaining, and savings progress. Category spending breakdown with visual charts.

Formulas are built in — numbers update automatically as you enter data. No manual calculations needed.
