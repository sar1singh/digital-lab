# Nivora Invoice Generator & Payment Tracker — Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

1. **Open in Google Sheets**: Click the copy link to create your own editable copy in Google Drive. OR open the XLSX file in Microsoft Excel.

2. **Start Here tab**: Read the setup instructions on the first tab.

3. **Settings tab first**: Set your currency, default tax rate, and preferred dropdown values.

4. **Add clients**: Go to the Clients tab and enter your client information.

5. **Create invoices**: Use the Invoice Generator tab to create printable invoices.

6. **Track payments**: Enter payments in the Payment Tracker tab.

7. **View dashboard**: The Dashboard tab shows your revenue, outstanding balances, and invoice status breakdown automatically.

## Important Notes

- **Do not edit gray cells** — these contain formulas that calculate automatically.
- **Edit white/green cells** — these are input cells where you enter your data.
- **Sample data is included** — replace it with your own data. Delete or overwrite sample rows.
- **Backup your data** — keep a copy of your spreadsheet as you add data.
