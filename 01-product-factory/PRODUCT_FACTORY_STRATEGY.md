# Product Factory Strategy

Product Factory is the active execution track inside Digital-Lab.

Digital-Lab is not Product Factory. Digital-Lab remains the broader lab for digital business experiments. Product Factory is the current path because it is the fastest realistic route to first revenue.

## Primary Goal

Get first product built, first product listed, first product sale, and first $50-$100 revenue as quickly as possible.

## Success Metrics

- Products published
- Listings live
- First sale
- First $50 revenue
- First $100 revenue

## Operating Thesis

Small proven-format products can reach first revenue faster than SaaS, content, audience building, or infrastructure work.

The target product types are:
- Tiny utilities
- Spreadsheets
- Calculators
- Templates
- Business tools

## Do Not Optimize For

- SaaS
- Branding
- Logos
- Website building
- Newsletters
- Audience building
- Social media growth
- Infrastructure
- Automation for automation's sake

## Locked Decisions

- Product Factory is active.
- SaaS Lab is parked.
- Content Lab is parked.
- Agency Lab is parked.
- First revenue is more important than innovation.
- Compete in existing proven markets.
- Copy successful product formats legally.
- Improve packaging and execution.
- Build -> Publish -> Learn -> Repeat.

## Current Target Products

1. Freelancer Pricing Calculator
2. POD Profit Calculator

## Product Rules

- Build in 1-2 days.
- Use proven formats.
- Keep product scope narrow.
- Publish before improving.
- Sell on Gumroad or Payhip first.
- Track buyer behavior before expanding.
- Prefer usefulness over novelty.

## Default Price Range

- Single calculator/template: $5-$12
- Bundle after validation: $19-$29

## Anti-Drift Question

Before any task, ask: will this increase the probability of first $50-$100 revenue?

If no, park it.
