## Research Summary

Research areas completed:

- Gumroad
- Creative Market
- KDP
- Career market
- Freelancer market
- Small business market
- Teacher market
- Wedding market

Research conclusion: buyers spend money when ROI is obvious, time is saved, mistakes are reduced, or operations become easier.

Strongest signal: operational utilities.

Examples:
- Calculators
- Trackers
- Dashboards
- Spreadsheets
- Finance tools
- Pricing tools

## Product Factory Rules

Build many small products, not one large product.

Product pattern:
1. Build Product 1
2. Publish
3. Build Product 2
4. Publish
5. Build Product 3
6. Publish
7. Observe views, downloads, purchases, and feedback
8. Adjust

Products should be:
- Small
- Focused
- Useful
- Easy to explain
- Creatable in 1-2 days

Avoid:
- Huge dashboards
- Giant Notion systems
- SaaS MVPs
- Complex onboarding
