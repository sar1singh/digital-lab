# Utility Product Backlog V1

Last Updated: 2026-06-05

Goal: identify 30 small operational utility products, rank them for first-sale probability, and choose the first three products to build.

Revenue target: first $50-$100 outside salary.

Distribution: Gumroad and Payhip first. No website, Etsy shop, newsletter, or SaaS until validation.

## Scoring Method

Each candidate is scored from 1-5.

- Demand: visible buyer need and search/platform demand
- Competition: 5 means less crowded or easier to differentiate
- Build effort: 5 means easiest to build in 1-2 days
- First-sale probability: chance of a fast low-ticket sale
- Expansion potential: ability to become a bundle, toolkit, or next product

Total score is the sum of all five columns.

## Recommended First Three Builds

### Product 1: POD Profit and Pricing Calculator

Why first:
- Strongest match to operational utility demand
- Direct money outcome
- Easy to build in spreadsheet form
- Clear Gumroad/Payhip listing title
- Can expand into a POD seller toolkit

Minimum product:
- Cost inputs: base cost, shipping, packaging, platform fee, transaction fee, ad cost
- Pricing outputs: profit, margin, break-even price, target-margin price
- Scenario table for 5-10 price points
- Simple instructions tab
- Example filled-in tab

Suggested price: $5-$9.

### Product 2: Freelancer Project Pricing Calculator

Why second:
- User can build it fast
- Buyer problem is emotionally sharp: undercharging
- Good expansion into freelancer ops templates

Minimum product:
- Inputs: target hourly rate, estimated hours, revision buffer, expenses, tax reserve, desired margin
- Outputs: minimum quote, recommended quote, rush quote, margin, effective hourly rate
- Scope-risk checklist
- Example project scenarios

Suggested price: $5-$12.

### Product 3: Etsy Fee and Profit Calculator

Why third:
- Strong known demand
- Validates seller-tool appetite
- Can be sold without launching on Etsy first

Minimum product:
- Inputs: sale price, material cost, shipping charged, shipping cost, listing fee, transaction fee, payment fee, ads
- Outputs: net profit, margin, break-even price, target-margin price
- Multi-item table
- Clear disclaimer that fee structures can change and users should verify platform fees

Suggested price: $5-$9.

## Immediate Execution Plan

1. Build Product 1 as a polished spreadsheet.
2. Create a simple Gumroad/Payhip listing with plain utility-focused copy.
3. Publish before building Product 2.
4. Track views, downloads, purchases, and buyer questions.
5. Build Product 2 only after Product 1 is listed.

## Anti-Drift Rule

Do not create a brand, logo, website, newsletter, SaaS app, or content plan before Product 1 is published.

Ask before any new task: will this increase probability of first $50-$100 revenue?
